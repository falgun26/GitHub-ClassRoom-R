Lockdown! What is lockdown? Lockdown is a restriction policy for people or communities to stay where they are, usually due to specific risks to themselves or to others if they can move and interact freely. So we can guess how it is affected in life for local lives, especially poor countries and for their people. And the country loses normal economic earnings and reserves. Shortly, we can say it is like another pandemic into a pandemic.
